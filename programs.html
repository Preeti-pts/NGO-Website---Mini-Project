Our Programs

At Nav Disha, we are committed to creating sustainable and impactful change across communities. Our work focuses on four core areas that directly influence the quality of life for underprivileged individuals and families: Education, Medical Care, Environment, and Livelihood.

Education: Shiksha Disha

We believe education is the most powerful tool for change. Our education programs aim to bridge learning gaps and promote digital and academic literacy, especially in rural and marginalized communities.

  • After-school learning centers
  • Digital literacy programs for rural kids
  • Teacher training & capacity building
  • Mentorship for dropout prevention

We believe education is the most powerful tool for transformative change. At Shiksha Disha, our mission is to ensure that every child, regardless of their background or location, has access to quality learning opportunities. Our education programs are specially designed to bridge academic gaps and promote both digital and traditional literacy, with a strong focus on empowering students from rural and marginalized communities.
We operate after-school learning centers that provide a safe and supportive environment where children receive academic assistance, engage in creative activities, and build essential life skills. Our digital literacy programs introduce rural children to the world of technology, equipping them with foundational computer knowledge and internet navigation skills that are crucial in today’s digital age.
Through Shiksha Disha, we strive to build a future where every child has the tools, guidance, and opportunities they need to thrive.

Medical: Swasthya Disha

Health is a human right. Our medical initiatives provide essential healthcare access to underserved populations, ensuring healthier communities through preventive and curative care.

  • Regular health screening camps
  • Nutrition support (midday meals, supplements)
  • Menstrual hygiene awareness for adolescent girls
  • Mental health counseling for children & parents

At Swasthya Disha, we believe that health is a fundamental human right, not a privilege. Our medical initiatives are dedicated to ensuring that underserved and vulnerable communities have access to essential healthcare services. By focusing on both preventive and curative care, we strive to build healthier, more resilient communities, particularly in rural and marginalized areas where healthcare access is limited.
We regularly organize health screening camps that offer check-ups, diagnostic services, and medical consultations at no cost, helping to identify and address health issues at an early stage. Our nutrition support programs, including midday meals and dietary supplements, target malnutrition among children and expectant mothers, improving overall well-being and growth.
Through Swasthya Disha, we are committed to promoting a holistic approach to health—where physical, nutritional, and mental well-being are treated as interconnected priorities in creating a healthier future for all.

Livelihood: Kaushal Disha

Empowerment starts with opportunity. Our livelihood programs aim to make youth and women financially independent by equipping them with practical skills and entrepreneurship training.

  • Vocational training for women & youth
  • Entrepreneurship support & micro-financing
  • Financial literacy workshops
  • Digital marketing & freelancing courses

At Kaushal Disha, we believe that empowerment begins with opportunity and self-reliance. Our livelihood programs are designed to equip youth and women—especially those from economically disadvantaged backgrounds—with the skills, confidence, and resources needed to become financially independent and lead dignified lives.
We offer a wide range of vocational training programs tailored to local needs, including tailoring, beauty services, computer skills, electrical work, and more. These hands-on courses enable participants to secure employment or start their own small businesses. Our entrepreneurship support and micro-financing initiatives provide aspiring entrepreneurs with mentorship, business development guidance, and access to seed capital—helping turn ideas into income-generating ventures.
Through Kaushal Disha, we aim to foster a culture of self-sufficiency, innovation, and economic inclusion—unlocking the potential of every individual to thrive and contribute meaningfully to their families and communities.

Environment: Paryavaran Disha

Protecting the planet is vital for future generations. Our environmental efforts focus on awareness, conservation, and sustainable practices within local communities.

  • Community clean-up drives
  • Tree plantation & urban gardening
  • Waste management awareness
  • Eco-friendly product making workshops

At Paryavaran Disha, we recognize that protecting the environment is essential to securing a healthier, sustainable future for generations to come. Our initiatives are centered on creating awareness, encouraging conservation efforts, and promoting eco-friendly practices within local communities—especially in areas where environmental education and resources are limited.
We regularly conduct community clean-up drives to promote public hygiene and civic responsibility, transforming neglected spaces into clean and usable areas. Our tree plantation campaigns and urban gardening projects not only enhance green cover but also educate communities on the importance of biodiversity, local ecosystems, and climate resilience.
Through Paryavaran Disha, we strive to build a strong culture of environmental stewardship by involving people at the grassroots level. Our goal is to inspire collective action for conservation and sustainability—because every small step counts when it comes to protecting our planet.
