ABOUT US

In a world where opportunities are often unequal, Navi Disha was born out of a deep commitment to create positive change and empower the underserved. We are a grassroots NGO that believes every individual has the right to live with dignity, purpose, and hope. Navi Disha is not just an organization—it is a movement toward inclusive development and human upliftment. Founded by passionate changemakers, Navi Disha aims to bridge the gap between potential and opportunity. With every initiative, we strive to create a ripple effect of change—one life, one community at a time.

MISSION

At Navi Disha, our mission is to ignite hope, restore dignity, and enable sustainable development by guiding individuals and communities towards a new direction of growth, empowerment, and self-reliance. We exist to address the deep-rooted social, economic, and educational inequalities that hinder people from reaching their full potential.

VISION

we envision a just, inclusive, and empowered society where every individual—regardless of their background—has the opportunity to lead a life of dignity, purpose, and potential. We aim to create a future where children have access to quality education, women are uplifted and self-reliant, communities are healthy and self-sustaining, and development is both equitable and environmentally conscious.

INSPIRATION

The idea of Navi Disha was inspired by real stories of struggle, resilience, and hope. We observed how small efforts—like teaching a child, supporting a mother's small business, or offering health aid to the elderly—could bring about significant, lasting change. This realization led to the foundation of Navi Disha: a platform to nurture, uplift, and empower.

Recent Events & Initiatives

At Navi Disha, we are committed to creating a brighter, more equitable future for all. Through our recent initiatives and events, we've made strides towards positive change, and we invite YOU to be a part of this journey. Together, we can make an even bigger impact!

Annual Health & Wellness Camp

Date: April 15, 2025

We organized a comprehensive health camp offering free medical checkups, vaccinations, and health awareness sessions. Over 500 children and families received critical healthcare support.

Back-to-School Drive

Date: March 20, 2025

Distributed school kits including books, stationery, and uniforms to 300 underprivileged children, helping them start the academic year with confidence.

Women's Empowerment Workshop

Date: February 25, 2025

A skill-building workshop for 50 women focusing on vocational training, financial literacy, and self-confidence building. Empowering women, empowering communities!

Community Clean-Up Drive

Date: January 10, 2025

Volunteers and children joined hands to clean public spaces, plant trees, and promote environmental awareness. Together, we're making our communities healthier and greener.
